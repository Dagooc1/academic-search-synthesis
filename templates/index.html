How to Use for Academic Research

For Literature Reviews (RRL):
  1. Search your research topic
  2. Select relevant papers (high reliability scores)
  3. Click "Generate RRL" for formatted literature review
  4. Export citations for your reference manager
  5. Copy the generated RRL to your paper
For Research Papers:
  1. Find papers with complete abstracts
  2. Check PDF availability for full text
  3. Use multiple citation formats
  4. Export to BibTeX for LaTeX papers
  5. Generate summaries for each section
Research Tip:

Always verify information from original sources. This tool helps with discovery and organization, but critical evaluation is essential.
